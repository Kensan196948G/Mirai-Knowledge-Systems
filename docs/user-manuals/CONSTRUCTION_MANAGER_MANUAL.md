## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. **Access the System**
   - Open your web browser
   - Navigate to: `http://your-server:5100/login.html`

2. **Enter Credentials**
   - Username: Your assigned username
   - Password: Your password
   - Click "ログイン" (Login)

3. **First-Time Setup**
   - You will be prompted to change your password
   - Set up multi-factor authentication (recommended)

### Dashboard Overview

The main dashboard provides:
- **Recent Knowledge**: Latest approved knowledge entries
- **Project Status**: Active projects you're involved in
- **Pending Approvals**: Items awaiting your approval
- **Incident Alerts**: Recent safety incidents
- **Notifications**: System and personal notifications

### Navigation

- **ナレッジ管理 (Knowledge)**: Browse and create knowledge entries
- **プロジェクト (Projects)**: Manage project information
- **事故レポート (Incidents)**: Report and review safety incidents
- **専門家相談 (Consultations)**: Request expert advice
- **承認フロー (Approvals)**: Review and approve content
- **レポート (Reports)**: View analytics and reports

## Knowledge Management

### Browsing Knowledge

#### Search and Filter
1. Click "ナレッジ管理" in the navigation
2. Use the search bar for keyword search
3. Apply filters:
   - **カテゴリ (Category)**: 施工計画, 品質管理, 安全衛生, etc.
   - **ステータス (Status)**: ドラフト, 承認済み, アーカイブ
   - **更新日 (Updated Date)**: Last 7 days, 30 days, etc.

#### Reading Knowledge Entries
- Click on any knowledge entry to view details
- Related knowledge is shown on the right sidebar
- Rate the usefulness with the star rating
- Add to favorites for quick access

### Creating Knowledge

#### When to Create Knowledge
Create knowledge entries when you discover:
- New construction techniques or methods
- Lessons learned from projects
- Best practices that should be shared
- Solutions to common problems

#### Creating a New Entry

1. **Access Create Form**
   - Click "ナレッジ管理" → "新規作成" (New)

2. **Fill Required Fields**
   - **タイトル (Title)**: Clear, descriptive title
   - **概要 (Summary)**: Brief description (max 500 characters)
   - **内容 (Content)**: Detailed information
   - **カテゴリ (Category)**: Select appropriate category
   - **タグ (Tags)**: Add relevant keywords (max 20)

3. **Optional Fields**
   - **プロジェクト (Project)**: Link to specific project
   - **優先度 (Priority)**: High, Medium, Low

4. **Submit for Approval**
   - Click "下書き保存" (Save Draft) or "承認依頼" (Submit for Approval)
   - Quality assurance officers will review your submission

#### Best Practices for Content Creation
- **Be Specific**: Include concrete details, measurements, procedures
- **Use Examples**: Reference actual projects or situations
- **Include Visuals**: Add diagrams, photos, or sketches when helpful
- **Cite Sources**: Reference regulations, standards, or expert opinions
- **Keep Updated**: Review and update content regularly

### Managing Your Knowledge

#### Editing Entries
- Only edit entries you've created or have permission to modify
- All changes are tracked in the revision history
- Major changes may require re-approval

#### Tracking Usage
- View view counts and ratings for your entries
- Monitor which entries are most helpful to others
- Use feedback to improve content quality

## Incident Management

### Understanding Incident Types

The system tracks various types of incidents:
- **軽微 (Minor)**: Near misses, minor injuries
- **中程度 (Moderate)**: Medical treatment required
- **重大 (Serious)**: Serious injuries, property damage
- **致命的 (Fatal)**: Fatalities

### Reporting Incidents

#### When to Report
Report incidents immediately when:
- Safety incidents occur
- Near misses are observed
- Quality issues are discovered
- Equipment failures happen
- Process deviations occur

#### Incident Reporting Process

1. **Access Incident Form**
   - Click "事故レポート" → "新規報告" (New Report)

2. **Fill Incident Details**
   - **タイトル (Title)**: Clear description of incident
   - **説明 (Description)**: Detailed account of what happened
   - **発生日 (Incident Date)**: When it occurred
   - **場所 (Location)**: Where it happened
   - **重大度 (Severity)**: Select appropriate level
   - **ステータス (Status)**: Investigation in progress

3. **Root Cause Analysis**
   - **根本原因 (Root Cause)**: Primary cause of incident
   - **対策 (Countermeasures)**: Actions to prevent recurrence

4. **Submit Report**
   - Submit for review by safety officers
   - You'll be notified of approval status

#### Best Practices for Incident Reporting
- **Report Immediately**: Don't delay reporting
- **Be Factual**: Stick to facts, avoid speculation
- **Include Details**: Describe sequence of events, conditions, people involved
- **Suggest Improvements**: Include ideas for prevention
- **Follow Up**: Monitor the investigation and implementation of countermeasures

### Reviewing Incidents

#### Incident List View
- View all incidents with filtering options
- Sort by date, severity, or status
- Search by keywords or location

#### Incident Details
- Full incident description
- Investigation findings
- Corrective actions taken
- Lessons learned and shared knowledge

## Expert Consultation

### When to Request Consultation

Request expert consultation when you encounter:
- Complex technical problems
- Unusual site conditions
- Regulatory interpretation questions
- Design or specification issues
- Quality control concerns

### Creating Consultation Requests

1. **Access Consultation Form**
   - Click "専門家相談" → "新規相談" (New Consultation)

2. **Define the Problem**
   - **タイトル (Title)**: Clear problem statement
   - **説明 (Description)**: Detailed description of the issue
   - **優先度 (Priority)**: Urgent, High, Medium, Low
   - **専門分野 (Specialization)**: Relevant expertise area

3. **Provide Context**
   - **プロジェクト (Project)**: Link to related project
   - **添付資料 (Attachments)**: Upload photos, documents, drawings
   - **期限 (Deadline)**: When you need the response

4. **Submit Request**
   - System assigns appropriate expert
   - You'll receive notifications on progress

### Managing Consultations

#### Tracking Status
- **Open**: Awaiting expert assignment
- **Assigned**: Expert assigned, consultation in progress
- **In Progress**: Active discussion with expert
- **Resolved**: Problem solved, solution provided
- **Closed**: Consultation completed

#### Communication with Experts
- Use the consultation interface for Q&A
- Upload additional information as needed
- Rate the expert's response quality
- Mark consultation as resolved when satisfied

## Approval Workflows

### Understanding Approval Process

Content goes through approval workflow to ensure:
- **Accuracy**: Information is technically correct
- **Completeness**: All required details are included
- **Compliance**: Meets regulatory requirements
- **Quality**: Professional presentation and usefulness

### Your Approval Responsibilities

As construction managers, you may be asked to approve:
- Knowledge entries from your team
- Incident reports from your projects
- Project-related documentation

### Approval Process

1. **Review Pending Items**
   - Click "承認フロー" in navigation
   - View items awaiting your approval

2. **Review Content**
   - Read the full content carefully
   - Check for technical accuracy
   - Verify compliance with standards
   - Assess completeness and clarity

3. **Make Decision**
   - **承認 (Approve)**: Content meets standards
   - **却下 (Reject)**: Content needs revision
     - Provide specific feedback on required changes

4. **Add Comments**
   - Include constructive feedback
   - Suggest improvements or additional information

### Approval Best Practices

#### Review Criteria
- **Technical Accuracy**: Information is correct and current
- **Practical Value**: Useful for field application
- **Clarity**: Easy to understand and follow
- **Completeness**: All necessary details included
- **Safety**: Considers safety implications

#### Providing Feedback
- Be specific about issues found
- Suggest concrete improvements
- Encourage good practices
- Acknowledge good work
